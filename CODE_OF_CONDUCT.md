# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free
experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Give specific, actionable feedback.
- Discuss linguistic questions with care and neutrality.
- Respect that Hindi and its vocabulary are historically layered.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Political or identity-based attacks.
- Purity rhetoric or hostile framing of language communities.
- Personal attacks in issues, discussions, or pull requests.
- Spam or intentionally disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block
participants who violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you see behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please open a private
communication channel with the maintainer where available, or report it through
GitHub's platform tools.
